FAQ

What level is this pack for?

Designed for intermediate to advanced guitarists who already know basic scales, pentatonics, and simple techniques but want to expand their soloing vocabulary.

Are the licks just meant to be memorized?

No. Study the phrase, understand its sound, practice it in context, then adapt it to create your own variations.

Does the pack include spoken lessons?

The focus is demonstration. Each phrase is shown on video at normal and slow speeds with on-screen tabs and supporting files.

Do I need Guitar Pro?

No. Guitar Pro files are optional — they’re useful for looping and precise tempo control. Videos and PDFs are included for every phrase.

Do I need a lot of music theory?

No. Basic technique and familiarity with scales help, but the pack is practical: you learn musical phrases that you can apply immediately.
